Curious Human Being

My name is Lisa Sy. I am a designer, artist, & front-end developer based in the San Francisco Bay Area. I am a product designer at Facebook.

I'm passionate about empowering people to understand each other and themselves in compassionate and honest ways. I design products and experiences towards this mission. I use art to share stories with one another.

Previously, I was a product designer at thoughtbot in San Francisco. At thoughtbot, I organized a product design talk series called Taco Tuesdays. I've provided mentorship to designers and engineers assisting them in building their careers.

I graduated from Wesleyan University with an American Studies degree, concentrating in Race and Ethnic Studies. At Wesleyan, I co-founded and ran a student-run design and branding consultancy called TypeClub.

I'm proud to call Oakland, California my home. When I'm not designing, you can find me sketching, going to music shows, enjoying the sunshine at Lake Merritt, writing, practicing Cantonese and French, and learning about the world and people through stories.
