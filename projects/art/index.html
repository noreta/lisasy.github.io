Background

All my life, I've loved art. I enjoy the practice of creating 2D and 3D forms from my imagination. The nature of art is that it brings the artist's subjectives observations, opinions, and lived bodily experience into the art they make.

Ways of expressing

While I've experimented with a lot of different medium, my favorite is probably the pencil. It's one of the simplest tools, yet it can express so much.

The sketchbook

All artists ought to carry a sketchbook around with them everywhere they go. It's important to see many different things and places so that we can find inspiration and understand connections between things. Sketchbooks allow us to capture ideas and thoughts in the moment. We work within and on the process when we use our sketchbooks.

Oils

Something I want to get back into doing more of is oil painting. It's been a little bit difficult to find the studio space in the Bay Area, but it will work out.
